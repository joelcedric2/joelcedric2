### Commercial products (private repos)

- **YSI** ([ysisafe.com](https://ysisafe.com)) — Lets AI agents spend money safely. Non-custodial payment rails where autonomous agents request crypto transactions through Safe multisig wallets. A 4-layer risk engine stops bad transactions before they hit the chain.
- **AgroEng** — Tells farmers what's wrong with their crops before they lose the harvest. 22-disease classifier for West African staple crops — 85% accuracy, 99% top-3, runs offline on a phone (~10 MB).
- **PRISM** — Brings payments, identity verification, and compliance to markets that don't have them. Country-agnostic financial infrastructure, currently validating in Nigeria.
- **PoliCit** — Gives civilians a direct, accountable line to law enforcement. Incident reports, SOS alerts, and misconduct reporting flow through rank-aware dashboards up to the Inspector General.
